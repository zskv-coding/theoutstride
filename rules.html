üéÆ General Rules

1. Be Respectful

Treat all players and staff with respect. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind will not be tolerated.

2. No Cheating or Hacking

The use of hacked clients, x-ray texture packs, or any unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. This includes auto-clickers, kill aura, fly hacks, etc.

3. No Griefing

Do not destroy, modify, or steal from other players' builds without permission. Respect others' property and creations.

4. Keep Chat Clean

No excessive swearing, spam, advertising, or inappropriate content in chat. Keep conversations friendly and appropriate for all ages.

5. No Exploiting

Do not exploit bugs, glitches, or duplication methods. Report any bugs you find to staff immediately.

6. English Only in Global Chat

Please use English in global chat to ensure staff can moderate effectively. You may use other languages in private messages.

3. Claim Your Land

Use the claiming system to protect your builds. Staff cannot restore unclaimed builds that are griefed.

4. Respect Build Proximity

Do not build too close to other players without permission. Give others space for their projects.
