üéÆ General Guidelines

1. Be Respectful

Treat all creators with respect. We're here to support each other and create great content together. Keep things positive and drama-free.

2. No Cheating

Play fair! No x-ray, duplication glitches, or other exploits. If you find a bug, report it to the admin team.

3. Respect Builds & Property

Don't grief, steal, or modify others' builds without permission. Pranks are encouraged, but they should be fun and reversible!

4. Content-Friendly Environment

Keep chat and builds appropriate for content creation. Remember that many of us create family-friendly content.

5. Report Issues

If you encounter bugs, performance issues, or problems with the modpack, let the admin team know so we can fix them.

6. Have Fun!

This is a creative space for making great content and building friendships. Enjoy the experience!

1. Build Responsibly

Keep builds content-appropriate and avoid anything offensive. We want everyone to feel comfortable featuring the server in their videos.

2. Optimize Your Builds

Be mindful of server performance. Avoid excessive entities, lag machines, or overly complex redstone that could impact others' gameplay.

3. Respect Personal Space

Give other creators space for their bases and projects. Coordinate with neighbors before building nearby.

4. Shopping District

Follow community guidelines for the shopping district. Keep shops active and fairly priced to maintain a healthy economy.

2. Collaborative Projects

3. Storylines & Lore

Feel free to create storylines and lore, but make sure they're inclusive and don't negatively impact others' content.

4. Spoilers

Be mindful of spoilers in chat. Not everyone is caught up on everyone else's content!
