Server Guidelines

Community standards for TheOutstride

These guidelines help maintain a positive, collaborative environment for all creators on TheOutstride.

General Guidelines

Be Respectful

Treat all creators with respect. We're here to support each other and create great content together. Keep things positive and drama-free.

No Cheating

Play fair. No x-ray, duplication glitches, or exploits. If you find a bug, report it to the admin team.

Respect Builds & Property

Don't grief, steal, or modify others' builds without permission. Pranks are encouraged, but they should be fun and reversible.

Content-Friendly Environment

Keep chat and builds appropriate for content creation. Many of us create family-friendly content.

Report Issues

If you encounter bugs, performance issues, or problems with the modpack, let the admin team know.

Building & Gameplay

Build Responsibly

Keep builds content-appropriate and avoid anything offensive. We want everyone to feel comfortable featuring the server in their videos.

Optimize Your Builds

Be mindful of server performance. Avoid excessive entities, lag machines, or overly complex redstone.

Respect Personal Space

Give other creators space for their bases and projects. Coordinate with neighbors before building nearby.

Shopping District

Follow community guidelines for the shopping district. Keep shops active and fairly priced.

Content Creation

Support Each Other

Promote and support fellow creators' content. We grow together.

Collaborative Projects

Community builds and events are encouraged. Coordinate with others for collaborations.

Storylines & Lore

Feel free to create storylines and lore, but make sure they're inclusive and don't negatively impact others' content.

Spoilers

Be mindful of spoilers in chat. Not everyone is caught up on everyone else's content.

Pranks & PvP

Pranks Are Encouraged

Pranks are a fun part of the SMP experience. Keep them creative, reversible, and in good spirit.

PvP Guidelines

PvP should be consensual unless part of an agreed-upon event or storyline. Don't interrupt others' content recording.

Prank Cleanup

If asked, help clean up your pranks. The goal is fun, not frustration.

These guidelines help maintain a positive, creative environment. Use common sense and remember we're all here to create great content and have fun together. When in doubt, communicate with your fellow creators.
