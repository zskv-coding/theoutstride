About TheOutstride

A private content creator SMP inspired by HermitCraft

Our Story

TheOutstride is a private, whitelist-only Minecraft SMP server created for content creators who want to collaborate, build, and create entertaining content together. Inspired by servers like HermitCraft, we've built a community focused on creativity, collaboration, and quality content creation.

Our server features a carefully curated custom modpack that enhances the vanilla Minecraft experience without overwhelming it. We believe in letting creativity shine while adding just enough quality-of-life improvements and exciting new features to keep things fresh and interesting.

What Makes Us Special

  • Carefully selected content creators who bring unique perspectives and styles
  • Custom modpack designed to enhance without overwhelming the vanilla experience
  • Collaborative projects and community builds
  • Regular server events and storylines
  • Drama-free, supportive environment for all creators
  • Shopping district and player-driven economy
  • Season-based format with fresh starts and new opportunities
  • Focus on quality content and genuine friendships

Our Mission

At TheOutstride, our mission is to provide a creative, collaborative space where content creators can thrive and produce amazing content for their audiences. We strive to:

  • Foster a positive, drama-free environment for all creators
  • Support each other's content and help channels grow
  • Create engaging storylines and collaborative projects
  • Maintain excellent server performance and stability
  • Balance modded features with vanilla Minecraft charm
  • Build lasting friendships within the creator community

The Modpack

Our custom modpack is designed to enhance the Minecraft experience while keeping the game's core identity intact. We've carefully selected mods that add quality-of-life improvements, new building options, and exciting gameplay mechanics without making the game feel overwhelming or unrecognizable.

The modpack includes improvements to building, farming, exploration, and more - all balanced to work together seamlessly. We regularly update and refine the modpack based on creator feedback to ensure the best possible experience.

Watch Our Creators

TheOutstride is a private, invite-only server for content creators. While you can't join the server directly, you can follow along with all the amazing content being created! Check out our creators' channels to watch their adventures, builds, and stories unfold.
