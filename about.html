About TheOutstride

A private content creator SMP

What is TheOutstride?

TheOutstride is a private, whitelist-only Minecraft SMP server for content creators. Inspired by servers like HermitCraft, we've built a community focused on creativity, collaboration, and quality content.

Our server features a custom modpack that enhances the vanilla Minecraft experience without overwhelming it. We believe in letting creativity shine while adding quality-of-life improvements and new features to keep things interesting.

What Makes Us Different

  • Carefully selected content creators with unique styles
  • Custom modpack designed to enhance without overwhelming
  • Collaborative projects and community builds
  • Regular server events and storylines
  • Drama-free, supportive environment
  • Shopping district and player-driven economy
  • Season-based format with fresh starts
  • Focus on quality content and genuine friendships

Our Mission

At TheOutstride, our mission is to provide a creative space where content creators can thrive and produce great content for their audiences.

  • Foster a positive, drama-free environment
  • Support each other's content and help channels grow
  • Create engaging storylines and collaborative projects
  • Maintain excellent server performance
  • Balance modded features with vanilla charm
  • Build lasting friendships within the community

The Modpack

Our custom modpack enhances Minecraft while keeping its core identity intact. We've selected mods that add quality-of-life improvements, new building options, and gameplay mechanics without making the game feel overwhelming.

The modpack includes improvements to building, farming, exploration, and more - all balanced to work together. We regularly update based on creator feedback.

Watch Our Creators

TheOutstride is invite-only for content creators. While you can't join directly, you can follow along with all the content being created! Check out our creators' channels to watch their adventures and builds.
