About TheOutstride

A private content creator SMP

What is TheOutstride?

TheOutstride is a private Minecraft SMP for content creators. Think HermitCraft vibes - we're all about creativity, working together, and making good content.

We run a custom modpack that adds some nice features without going overboard. The goal is to keep the vanilla feel while throwing in quality-of-life stuff and some new mechanics to keep things fresh.

What Makes Us Different

  • Handpicked creators with different styles
  • Custom modpack that doesn't go crazy with features
  • Big collaborative projects and community builds
  • Server events and ongoing storylines
  • No drama, just people making content
  • Shopping district with player economy
  • Seasons that reset for fresh starts
  • Actually care about making good content

What We're Trying to Do

Basically, we want a place where creators can make content without dealing with BS. That means:

  • Keep things positive and drama-free
  • Help each other grow our channels
  • Work on cool projects together
  • Keep the server running smoothly
  • Balance mods with vanilla gameplay
  • Build actual friendships, not just content

The Modpack

We put together a custom modpack that adds stuff without ruining what makes Minecraft good. Quality-of-life improvements, new building blocks, some gameplay tweaks - nothing too crazy.

It's got improvements for building, farming, exploring, and other stuff. We update it when creators ask for changes or when something's broken.

Can I Join?

TheOutstride is invite-only for content creators. You can't join the server, but you can watch what we're doing. Check out our creators' channels to see their builds and whatever chaos is happening.
