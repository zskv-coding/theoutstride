About TheOutstride

A private content creator SMP

What is this?

TheOutstride is a private Minecraft server where content creators hang out and make videos. If you've seen HermitCraft, it's kinda like that - bunch of people building stuff, collaborating on projects, and just having fun.

We're running a custom modpack. Nothing too wild, just some QOL improvements and a few extras to keep gameplay interesting without straying too far from vanilla.

What makes us different

  • Small group of handpicked creators
  • Custom modpack (not too heavy, not too light)
  • Big community projects and collabs
  • Server-wide events and storylines
  • Zero tolerance for drama
  • Shopping district with its own economy
  • Seasonal resets to keep things fresh
  • Everyone actually cares about their content

Our goals

We wanted a server where creators could focus on making content without the usual headaches:

  • Keep the vibe positive
  • Support each other's channels
  • Build cool stuff together
  • Maintain stable server performance
  • Find the sweet spot between modded and vanilla
  • Make actual friends, not just networking contacts

About the modpack

Our modpack is custom-built to enhance gameplay without completely changing it. We've added quality-of-life features, new building options, and some gameplay tweaks that make sense.

Covers building, farming, exploration, and more. We tweak it based on feedback from the creators or when something breaks.

Can I join?

Nope, sorry. TheOutstride is invite-only for content creators. But you can still watch everything that happens - just check out our creators' channels to see what they're up to.
