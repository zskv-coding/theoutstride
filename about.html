About TheOutstride

Learn more about our server and community

Our Story

TheOutstride was founded with a simple vision: to create the ultimate Minecraft experience where players can explore, build, and thrive together. What started as a small community has grown into one of the most vibrant and welcoming Minecraft servers around.

We believe in creating a space where creativity knows no bounds, where friendships are forged, and where every player can find their place. Whether you're a builder, a fighter, a trader, or an explorer, TheOutstride has something special for you.

What Makes Us Different

  • Custom-built plugins designed specifically for our community
  • Regular events and competitions with amazing prizes
  • Fair and balanced gameplay with active anti-cheat systems
  • Dedicated staff team available 24/7
  • Regular updates and new content additions
  • Player-driven economy and marketplace
  • Cross-platform support (Java & Bedrock)
  • Active Discord community with thousands of members

Our Mission

At TheOutstride, our mission is to provide a safe, fun, and engaging environment for Minecraft players of all ages and skill levels. We strive to:

  • Foster a positive and inclusive community
  • Continuously improve and innovate our gameplay experience
  • Listen to our players and implement their feedback
  • Maintain the highest standards of server performance and security
  • Create memorable experiences that keep players coming back
